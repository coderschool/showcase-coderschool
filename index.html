OUR TRAINING PRINCIPLES

Students must pass a rigorous selection process to be admitted to any of 8 week classes. During the classes, engineers submit weekly projects for review and work with other top engineers and designers. The program concludes with a demo day where the top group projects are judged by a panel of notable engineering and startup leaders. Courses will be taught in English.
